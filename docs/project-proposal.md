# Project Proposal: Middle Earth Tracker

## Project Name
Middle Earth Tracker

## Target Audience
Fans of J.R.R. Tolkien's Lord of the Rings and Hobbit stories who want to track their personal progress through the books and films.

## Problem Statement
Tolkien fans often jump between the books and movies in different orders and have no simple way to track what they have read or watched, rate each entry, or see how far through the full Middle Earth saga they are.

## Value Proposition
My app helps Tolkien fans track their progress through the complete Middle Earth saga by giving them a personal dashboard to mark each book and movie as not started, in progress, or completed, and rate and review each one.

---

## Must-Have Features (MVP)

1. **User registration and login** — each visitor creates their own account so progress is saved per person
2. **Full catalog view** — display all 10 items (4 books, 6 movies) organized by series
3. **Progress tracking** — mark each item as Not Started / In Progress / Completed
4. **Star ratings** — rate each item 1–5 stars after completing it
5. **Personal dashboard** — show overall completion percentage and series-level progress

## Explicitly Not Building (yet)

- Theatrical vs. extended edition tracking
- Chapter-by-chapter reading progress for books
- Social/friend features (following other users)
- Spoiler-tagged reviews visible to others
- Mobile app
